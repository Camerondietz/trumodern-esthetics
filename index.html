FAQ

Questions, answered.

How do I book an appointment?

All bookings are managed through our secure Square portal. Click any “Book” button to view live availability, select a service, and reserve your time.

What should I do before my first facial?

Arrive with clean skin if possible, avoid retinol or strong actives 48 hours prior, and bring a list of your current products. We’ll handle the rest.

Is there downtime after a Hydrafacial or peel?

Hydrafacials have zero downtime. Light chemical peels may produce mild flaking for 3–5 days. Your protocol is matched to your schedule and skin tolerance.

Do you offer packages or memberships?

Yes — series pricing for peels and Hydrafacials is available through Square. Memberships are introduced after your first treatment so we can tailor them to your goals.

What is your cancellation policy?

We kindly ask for at least 24 hours notice. Cancellations within that window may be subject to a 50% fee, in keeping with our small-studio model.

Can I bring a friend?

Of course — we love hosting two. Please book back-to-back appointments or message us to arrange a side-by-side experience.
